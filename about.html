Our Story

Unadulterated raw honey and traditional sun-cured pickles. Born from a mother's search for wellness.

A Mother's Mission

During the early years of my daughter, it was a regular routine of mine to visit doctors because of cough, fever, and sore throat. Standard medicines immediately treat the symptoms but do not allow the child's natural immunity system to build properly.

Desperate for a natural alternative, I remembered my grandmother's advice: a teaspoon of pure, unadulterated honey can cure organically and boost the immunity system in children above 3 years of age.

Fascinated by her rapid recovery after a spoonful of raw, local honey, I started learning more about the remarkable antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties that make honey a natural remedy for sore throats and minor ailments.

Sharing Nature's Remedy

What began as a personal mission to comfort my sick child organically evolved into a source of wellness for my entire family. After traveling extensively in search of pure, unadulterated honey, I eventually built a thriving organic honey and food business to share nature's remedies with others.

Mamafarm is a premium wellness brand owned and marketed by Srinika Agro Foods. We specialize in organic, unadulterated honey, sun-cured traditional pickles, and naturally brewed vinegar. Our diverse product range is inspired by uncompromising quality and great taste.
